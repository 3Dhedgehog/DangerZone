DangerZone

What's DangerZone?

Our app is the answer to global issues like epidemics, natural disasters, saturation, terrorist attacks, etc. It allows the community to mark places as dangerous, so you can avoid them. Don’t waste your time and try DangerZone for free.

How to use it?

You just have to enable geolocalisation and check the map. It's that simple! Dangerous places are grouped in following categories:

Categories

  • Epidemics

  • Saturation terrorist attacks

  • Natural disasters

  • Environmental pollutions

  • Wars
